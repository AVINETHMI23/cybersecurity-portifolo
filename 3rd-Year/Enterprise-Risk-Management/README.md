# Enterprise Information Security Risk Management (ISRM) Framework

## 🏢 Portfolio Summary
An executive-level operational risk assessment and architectural governance strategy designed to protect corporate digital infrastructure from emerging cyber threats. This report applies rigorous government and industry standards to align technical controls with corporate risk tolerance goals.

## 📋 Security Frameworks Applied
* **NIST SP 800-39:** Structured the overarching organizational risk context, modeling strategic business issues, threats, and corporate asset prioritization matrices.
* **NIST SP 800-37 / 800-53:** Engineered defensive control profiles spanning access authentication mechanisms (Microsoft Entra ID MFA), perimeter defense arrays (FortiGate 40F infrastructure deployments), and automated endpoint security.
* **Budgeting & Governance:** Formulated detailed technology procurement projections, policy implementation guidelines, and risk tolerance boundaries to bridge raw engineering metrics with corporate leadership goals.
